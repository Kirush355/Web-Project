Information about the sanctuary;
The **Animal Ark Wildlife Sanctuary** was established in 1995 by **David Mwangi** and **Lilian Atieno** in **Laikipia, Kenya**. Initially founded as a refuge for injured and orphaned wildlife, the sanctuary has grown into a well-respected conservation facility dedicated to the protection, rehabilitation, and preservation of wild animals. Covering over 50 acres, Animal Ark Wildlife Sanctuary provides a safe, natural habitat for a variety of species, including cheetahs, elephants, birds, and foxes. Conservation efforts;
Animal Ark Wildlife Sanctuary is committed to conservation through rescue, rehabilitation, and educational initiatives. The sanctuary collaborates with Kenya Wildlife Service (KWS) and other conservation organizations to offer a permanent home for animals that cannot survive in the wild due to injuries or habitat loss. One of its major initiatives includes the **Cheetah Protection Program**, which aims to safeguard cheetah populations in Kenya. The sanctuary also promotes habitat restoration, sustainable eco-practices, and research to support conservation strategies.
